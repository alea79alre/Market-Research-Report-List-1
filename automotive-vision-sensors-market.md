<p><strong>The Automotive Vision Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocular Vision</li><li>Binocular (stereo) Vision</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive vision sensors market is primarily divided into monocular and binocular (stereo) vision types. Monocular vision systems use a single camera to capture images, relying on image processing algorithms to interpret the environment, making them less complex and cost-effective. In contrast, binocular vision systems utilize two cameras to create depth perception through stereoscopic imaging, enabling better object detection and spatial awareness. Both types play a crucial role in advancing autonomous driving technologies and enhancing driver-assistance features.</p></p>
