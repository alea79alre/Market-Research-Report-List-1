<p><p>Macro zoom microscopes are classified into two main types: automatic and stereo. Automatic macro zoom microscopes feature motorized zoom and focus adjustments, offering precise imaging with minimal user intervention, making them ideal for laboratory and industrial applications. Stereo macro zoom microscopes, on the other hand, provide a three-dimensional view of specimens, allowing for detailed observation and manipulation under low magnification. Both types cater to various fields, including biology, materials science, and electronics, enhancing the versatility and functionality of microscopic inspection.</p></p>
