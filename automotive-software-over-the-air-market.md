<p><p>The Automotive Software Over The Air (OTA) market comprises two primary types: Cellular and Wi-Fi. Cellular OTA enables updates via mobile networks, providing broader coverage and remote access, ideal for vehicles on the move. Wi-Fi OTA, on the other hand, allows updates when vehicles are parked near a Wi-Fi source, ensuring faster data transfer and saving on cellular data costs. Both methods are essential for enhancing vehicle performance, safety, and user experience through timely software updates and feature enhancements.</p></p>
