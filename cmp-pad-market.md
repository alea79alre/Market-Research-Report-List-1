<p><p>The CMP pad market is categorized into three main types: Polymer CMP pads, Non-woven CMP pads, and Composite CMP pads. Polymer CMP pads are made from synthetic materials, providing consistency and durability for chemical-mechanical polishing. Non-woven CMP pads, composed of layered fibers, offer flexibility and excellent surface conformability. Composite CMP pads combine various materials, optimizing performance for specific applications by balancing properties like hardness and abrasiveness. Each type caters to distinct needs in semiconductor and electronics manufacturing processes.</p></p>
