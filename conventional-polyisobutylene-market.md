<p><p>The conventional polyisobutylene market is categorized into three types based on molecular weight: low, medium, and high. Low molecular weight polyisobutylene is used in sealants, adhesives, and lubricants due to its flexibility and low viscosity. Medium molecular weight polyisobutylene offers improved viscosity and is utilized in products like adhesives and coatings. High molecular weight polyisobutylene is prized for its exceptional elasticity and strength, making it ideal for applications such as tires, automotive seals, and high-performance products.</p></p>
