<p><p>The Ethylene-Propylene Elastomers (EPM and EPDM) market can be categorized into two types: compatible and miscible. Compatible elastomers are those that can blend with other polymers without significant loss of properties, forming a stable mixture suitable for various applications. Miscible elastomers, on the other hand, mix at the molecular level, resulting in enhanced performance characteristics. Each type offers unique benefits, influencing their application in industries like automotive, construction, and consumer goods, where flexibility, durability, and weather resistance are essential.</p></p>
