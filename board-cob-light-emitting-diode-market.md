<p><p>The Board COB Light Emitting Diode (LED) market encompasses two main types: Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLEDs) and Inorganic Light Emitting Diodes (ILEDs). OLEDs utilize organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, offering enhanced flexibility, thinner designs, and vibrant colors, making them ideal for displays and lighting. In contrast, ILEDs are based on inorganic materials like gallium nitride, known for their high efficiency and durability, commonly used in general lighting and signage applications. Both technologies cater to diverse consumer demands.</p></p>
