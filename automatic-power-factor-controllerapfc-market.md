<p><p>Automatic Power Factor Controllers (APFC) are devices that automatically manage the power factor in electrical systems, optimizing the use of active power and reducing reactive power. The market for APFCs is categorized into two types: Active APFCs and Passive APFCs. Active APFCs utilize advanced electronic circuits for precise compensation and dynamic adjustment, while Passive APFCs rely on fixed capacitors and inductors for correction. Both types aim to enhance energy efficiency, reduce utility penalties, and streamline power consumption in industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
