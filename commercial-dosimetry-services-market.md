<p><ul><li>OSL Technology</li><li>TLD Technology</li><li>CR-39 Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Dosimetry Services market includes various technologies for measuring radiation exposure. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) technology utilizes special materials that luminesce when stimulated by light, providing accurate dose readings. TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimetry) employs crystals that emit light when heated, indicating the amount of radiation absorbed. CR-39 technology, a plastic polymer, detects alpha particles and other radiation, commonly used in personal dosimeters. Each technology offers distinct advantages in monitoring and quantifying radiation exposure in various settings.</p></p>
