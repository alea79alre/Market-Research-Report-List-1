<p><p>Automotive Multi Wheel Drive (AWD) systems enhance vehicle traction and stability by distributing power to all four wheels. Automatic AWD dynamically adjusts power distribution based on traction requirements, offering a balance between performance and fuel economy. Each type caters to diverse driving needs, influencing vehicle design and consumer preferences in the automotive market.</p></p>
