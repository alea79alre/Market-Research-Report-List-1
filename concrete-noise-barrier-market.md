<p><p>The concrete noise barrier market is divided into two primary types: ground-mounted and structure-mounted noise barriers. Ground-mounted noise barriers are installed on the ground, typically along highways or railways, providing substantial sound reduction by blocking noise from transportation sources. Structure-mounted noise barriers, on the other hand, are affixed to existing structures, such as buildings or bridges, serving a dual purpose of noise reduction and aesthetic enhancement. Both types are crucial in urban planning to mitigate noise pollution.</p></p>
