<p><p>The 2-Phase Stepper Motors market consists of two main types: bipolar and unipolar. Bipolar stepper motors utilize two coils and require a more complex driving circuit, providing greater torque and efficiency. They are preferred in high-performance applications. Unipolar stepper motors, on the other hand, have simpler circuitry with five or six leads and a more straightforward driving mechanism, making them easier to control. They are often used in applications that require simpler designs and lower precision.</p></p>
