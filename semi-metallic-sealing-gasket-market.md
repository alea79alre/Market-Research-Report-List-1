Round gaskets, often used in applications requiring uniform compression and sealing capability, are commonly found in flanged joints and pipe connections. Non-circular gaskets, including oval and rectangular shapes, cater to specialized applications with unique fitting requirements. Both types leverage the elastic and compressive properties of semi-metallic materials, enhancing sealing performance in high-temperature and pressure environments, making them essential in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
