<p><p>The anti-block additives market is primarily segmented into inorganic and organic types. Inorganic anti-block additives, such as silica or calcium carbonate, function by creating a barrier between layers of films or sheets, reducing adhesion and preventing sticking. Organic anti-block additives, including certain waxes and polymeric materials, offer similar benefits but often enhance surface appearance and processing characteristics. Both types are essential in applications like packaging, where maintaining product integrity and ease of handling is crucial.</p></p>
