<p><p>The nanofiltration elements market primarily consists of two main types: spiral membrane and tubular membrane. Spiral membranes are designed in a coiled configuration, maximizing surface area while minimizing space and pressure drop, making them ideal for various applications. Tubular membranes, on the other hand, feature a straight, cylindrical design that allows for easier cleaning and handling of higher suspended solids, often used in more complex processes. Both types are essential for effective separation in water treatment and industrial processes.</p></p>
