<p><strong>The Conventional Fire Alarm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Initiating Circuit</li><li>Multi-initiating Circuits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conventional fire alarm system market is categorized into two main types based on initiating circuits. A one initiating circuit system connects a single sensor or detector to the alarm panel, making it suitable for smaller buildings or specific areas. In contrast, a multi-initiating circuit system links multiple sensors across various zones, allowing for comprehensive monitoring and faster response in larger or complex structures. Both types serve to enhance fire safety, but their applications vary based on the scale and requirements of the installation.</p></p>
