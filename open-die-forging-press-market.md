<p><p>The open die forging press market is categorized into three main types: push-down forging presses, pull-down forging presses, and others. Push-down presses utilize a vertical motion to force the workpiece between dies, ensuring uniform deformation. Pull-down presses, on the other hand, pull the die down to shape the material, offering flexibility in designs. The "others" category includes alternative methods and technologies not classified under standard types, catering to specific applications and enhancing operational efficiency in forging processes.</p></p>
