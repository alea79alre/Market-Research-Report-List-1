<p><p>The flat welding flange market comprises two main types: plate flat welding flanges and flat welding flanges with necks. Plate flat welding flanges are typically used in applications requiring a flush surface and are welded directly to pipes. In contrast, flat welding flanges with necks feature an extended neck, providing greater strength and alignment during installation. Both types are essential in various industries for their ability to facilitate secure connections and support high-pressure systems, ensuring reliability and durability in piping applications.</p></p>
